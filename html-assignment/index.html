About Me

Hello everyone, my name is Daniela Gonzalez, but all my family and friends call me Dani which is the abbreviation for Daniela. I really like being called Dani, because in my country saying the full name is too formal, or when you meet a person for the first time. My pronouns are she/her, and I am 25 years old.

I am from Cali, Colombia, and it is known worldwide as the capital of Salsa, a typical dance of my country. In addition, it is a hot city, whose temperature does not vary much throughout the year, since it is a tropical country. So, moving to Canada, which is a cold country with seasons, I consider it as a new adaptation experience.

I have a degree in Business Administration, at the Icesi University on February 23rd, 2021. Because of my young age and being a recent graduate, my work experience is somehow limited. However, I worked as an intern in Brazil as responsible for suppliers and client service, and at a younger age, in Colombia, I started as an entrepreneur selling clothes through a catalogue which was very successful. I decided to apply for Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College, so I can enter the digital media industry to add that competitive advantage, and gain recognition from an international institution.
